About ISX

Who we are

ISX is the first Icelandic exchange market that offers trade in electronic coins, such as Auroracoin and Bitcoin for Icelandic krónur.

The ISX exchange market is designed with the best possible safety standards and methods. The purpose is to protect user information and security of underlying coins, as well as other funds, as well as user-friendly interface and secure business.

Secure exchange

User safety is our main priority. ISX is designed with the best safety standards and methods available. The purpose is to protect the user's information and the security of the underlying online bills, as well as other funds, as well as ensuring user-friendly interface and secure business.
